Potency/Repetition Guidance (Conservative, Single-Remedy):

- First prescription, stable chronic → 30C single dose; observe 5–7 days.
- Clear keynote picture with vitality → 200C single dose; observe 7–14 days.
- Children/elderly/sensitive → 6C/30C, single dose.
- No polypharmacy; do not repeat unless follow-up indicates.
- Follow-up: assess mental/emotional shift, generals, sleep, energy, appetite, stool, and the presenting complaint.
